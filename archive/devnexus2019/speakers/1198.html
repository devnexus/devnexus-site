If you want to talk about accessibility issues, we have to consider readability
The average adult in this country reads only at a 7th - 8th grade level, and when it comes to technical content, a persons reading level dramatically lowers. In other words, what you are writing on your websites might not be comprehendible by your audience. Find out how to determine the level of your website text and ways to revise to ensure your audience can read and comprehend your content.
